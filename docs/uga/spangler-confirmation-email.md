Drafts of both the EITS letter and the data retention / audit-logging policy that goes with it are in the project repository — I'm happy to send them as PDFs if that's easier, or we can find five minutes to walk through the high points.

Happy to take this in whatever direction works — a quick reply with a "go ahead" is enough; or if you'd rather meet briefly first, I can find time this week.
