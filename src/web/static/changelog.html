April 6, 2026 New feature

LaTeX & Math Support

You can now upload .tex files (or .zip archives with images and styles) and get back accessible PDFs. Equations render as SVG with MathML underneath, so screen readers can describe them. For complex math, the system writes a plain-language description of what the equation says. Algorithms, pseudocode, and TikZ diagrams come through too.

April 5, 2026 Quality improvement

Better Scanned PDF Results

Scanned PDFs now go through three steps: text extraction, structural analysis, and error correction. Tables, headings, and reading order in scanned documents are noticeably better. If you tried a scanned PDF earlier and weren't happy with the output, it's worth running it again.

April 5, 2026 Quality improvement

Visual Quality Checks

After remediating a scanned PDF, the system compares the original pages against the output to catch anything that got lost: missing text, dropped tables, content that shifted. Findings show up in your compliance report with side-by-side thumbnails.

February 24, 2026 New feature

Page-Based Pricing

Credits are now counted by page instead of by document. A 2-page assignment costs less than a 50-page course packet. You can see exactly how many pages you have left in the balance bar at the top of the page.

February 20, 2026 New feature

Password Reset

Forgot your password? There's now a "Forgot password?" link on the sign-in form that sends a reset link to your email. No more emailing support to get back into your account.
